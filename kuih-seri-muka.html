Kuih Seri Muka

A beloved traditional Malaysian kuih featuring a fragrant green pandan glutinous rice base topped with creamy white coconut custard.

Ingredients

For the Green Layer (Pandan Rice):

  • 250g glutinous rice, soaked for 2 hours
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 100ml water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 15–20 pandan leaves, blended with 100ml water, then strained

For the White Layer (Coconut Custard):

  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 100g sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp rice flour

Instructions

  1. Drain soaked glutinous rice and steam for 15 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Mix coconut milk, water, salt, and pandan juice. Add to steamed rice, stir well, and steam for another 20 minutes.
  3. Press rice mixture firmly into a greased 8x8 inch pan. Set aside.
  4. For custard: Beat eggs, then mix in coconut milk, sugar, salt, and rice flour until smooth.
  5. Strain custard mixture over the rice layer.
  6. Steam gently for 25–30 minutes until the custard is set (cover lid with cloth to prevent water droplets).
  7. Cool completely, then cut into bite-sized pieces.

Serve at room temperature for the best texture and flavor!

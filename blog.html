Why Preserve Family Recipes?

In a world of fast food and instant meals, home-cooked heritage dishes are at risk of being forgotten. At Rumah Rasa, we believe cooking is an act of love — and recipes are heirlooms.

Every dish tells a story: of grandmothers stirring sambal at dawn, of festivals filled with kuih, of simple meals that bring families together.

Explore our recipes and keep the tradition alive!

Rendang Ayam

A rich and aromatic Malaysian chicken curry, slow-cooked in coconut milk and spices until tender and deeply flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 lemongrass stalks, bruised
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Spice Paste (Ground Together):

  • 6 shallots
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 cm ginger
  • 2 cm galangal
  • 4 dried chilies (soaked)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Blend spice paste until smooth.
  2. Heat a pot and sauté the spice paste over medium heat for 5 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and stir to coat with the paste. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk, add kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, cinnamon, and star anise.
  5. Simmer uncovered on low heat for 45–60 minutes, stirring occasionally, until oil separates and gravy thickens.
  6. Add tamarind paste (if using) and salt. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or ketupat.

Rendang Ayam is perfect for festive occasions or family gatherings!

Teh Tarik

Malaysia’s iconic “pulled” tea – smooth, frothy, and perfectly sweetened with condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp strong black tea leaves (e.g., Boh or Ah Huat)
  • 200ml hot water
  • 2–3 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 1–2 tbsp evaporated milk (optional, for creaminess)

Instructions

  1. Brew the tea: Boil water and steep tea leaves for 3–5 minutes. Strain into a mug.
  2. Add condensed milk (and evaporated milk if using) to the hot tea. Stir well.
  3. Pour the tea back and forth between two mugs or a mug and a metal container from a height (about 30–50 cm). Repeat 5–10 times until frothy.
  4. Serve immediately while hot and foamy!

Pro tip: The higher you “pull”, the smoother and airier your Teh Tarik becomes!

About Rumah Rasa

Rumah Rasa (meaning "House of Flavors") was created to celebrate and preserve Malaysia’s rich culinary traditions. Our recipes come from real family kitchens — tested, loved, and shared across generations.

We do not represent any commercial entity. This website is a learning project for IML254 at Universiti Teknologi MARA.

Meet our team on the Team page, or learn more about our vision in our Blog.
